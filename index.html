About Us

What Do We Do?

 Here at Steel Frames Wales, we manufacture Light-Gauge Galvanised Steel Frames. 

90% of our frames are designed & built bespoke to each customer. 

Many of our customers are tradesmen, from all different backgrounds, looking to succeed in the Garden Room Market. We manufacture frames ready to erect, saving you: Time, Effort, and Costs!

Thanks to our consistent growth as a business, we are also able to offer; Design Consultations, Frame Delivery & Installations, Foundation Solutions, Fully-Finished Buildings, and much more. 

Why Choose Steel?

Why wouldn't you! Our Frames are made from Light-Gauge Galvanised Steel

Our Steel frames are quick to install, and unlike timber frames, steel does not twist, warp or burn. So once finished you can rest assured that your project is built to last

(Psst! Not sold yet? Steel is also much much cheaper than brick or timber!)

Who are We?

Here at Steel Frames Wales, we supply and fit light-gauge galvanised steel frames.

90% of our frames are tailor made for each of our customers, saving time, effort and money!

Process of Purchase

Here at Steel Frame's, we don't just guarantee a top-notch product for a top-notch price. Our Design Team are here to help from start to finish of your project.

  • Get in touch for a quick chat to weigh up your options and discuss the product, lead-times, and ballpark costs.
  • No matter how prepared you are... we can help! All we need is a rough sketch, a brief description of size and shape, or simply tell us your idea and we'll generate 3D Visuals, Plans, and a price in no time.
  • Once you're happy and raring to go, all we need is a signature and a deposit to begin production
  • Within 2-4 weeks your frame will be ready for delivery. Once on site most frames can be stood-up within 2 days.
  • Either us, you, or a third-party are welcome to finish the building complete with electrics, cladding, and a lick of paint!
